Mission

At the Alumni Association of Sri Shakthi Institute of Engineering and Technology, our mission is to foster a vibrant community of alumni who are engaged, connected, and empowered. We aim to create a platform that facilitates networking, collaboration, and support among our graduates, enabling them to contribute to the growth and development of the institute and society at large.

Vision

Our vision is to be a leading alumni association that inspires lifelong relationships among our members, promotes professional development, and enhances the reputation of Sri Shakthi Institute of Engineering and Technology. We strive to create opportunities for alumni to give back to their alma mater, mentor current students, and share their experiences and expertise.

Founding Story

The Alumni Association of Sri Shakthi Institute of Engineering and Technology was founded in 2006 with the vision of creating a strong network of graduates who could support each other and the institute. Recognizing the importance of maintaining connections after graduation, a group of passionate faculty members and alumni came together to establish a platform that would facilitate ongoing relationships.

From its humble beginnings, the association has grown into a thriving community that hosts various events, workshops, and networking opportunities. Our founding members believed in the power of collaboration and the impact that a united alumni network could have on both personal and professional growth. Today, we continue to build on that legacy, striving to create a supportive environment for all our members.

Join us in our journey to strengthen the bonds of our alumni community and contribute to the success of future generations of Sri Shakthi Institute of Engineering and Technology!
